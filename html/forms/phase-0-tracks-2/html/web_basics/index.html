The Odd Cat

This is the legend of Zuzu the Odd.

Why was she so odd, you ask?

  • Zuzu would often bark for no good reason.
  • Zuzu had a tendency to make daily bids for freedom.
  • Zuzu would often indulge in hunting human toes in the middle of the night, to her owners displeasure.

One day her owner did not shut the door all the way when leaving for work. Zuzu had a choice to make:

  1. To make her first successful bid for freedom.
  2. To go back to bed and nap for eight hours.

Zuzu wisely chose option two and lived happily ever after.
